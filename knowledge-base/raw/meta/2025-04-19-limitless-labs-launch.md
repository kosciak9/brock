# Limitless Labs Launch

- Source: https://limitlesstcg.com/limitless-labs-launch
- Collected: 2026-05-26
- Published: 2025-04-19

## Excerpts

Byline excerpt: Robin Schulz • April 19, 2025

"We’ve recently launched a new spin-off site, the Limitless Labs!"

"Starting this season, tournament organizers of Regionals and Internationals have been publishing every player’s decklist after the tournament (instead of just top players), which allows for way more insight into the metagame and players’ tournament runs than before."

"You can check out who and what decks someone played against, get a summary of the complete day 1 metagame, see matchup win rates, and more — but now for in-person majors instead of online tournaments!"

"Day 2 results ... will of course still be uploaded to our regular database, to support long-term archival, player profiles, and card database integrations, but if you want the most in-depth look possible into a single tournament, check out the Labs!"
