# Limitless Tools

- Source: https://limitlesstcg.com/tools
- Collected: 2026-05-26
- Published: Unknown

## Excerpts

The tools page documents several distinct player workflows:

- **Proxy Printer** for printing card images for testing and casual play
- **Swiss Calculator** for approximating Swiss-tournament results and decision points like intentional draws or drops
- **Image Generator** for turning a decklist into a shareable image
- **Opening Hand Calculator / DrawCalc** for opening-hand probabilities
- **Tabletop** for practicing opening hands and turns or playing complete games against yourself
