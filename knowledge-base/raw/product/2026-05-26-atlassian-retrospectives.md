# Atlassian sprint retrospectives

- Source: https://www.atlassian.com/team-playbook/plays/retrospective
- Collected: 2026-05-26
- Published: Unknown

## Excerpts

Page summary bullets:

- "Celebrate what went well during the last sprint."
- "Discuss what didn’t go as well or could have been better."
- "Determine how to make improvements in the next sprint."

The page frames retrospectives as a continuous-improvement tool and explicitly recommends:

- gathering feedback
- turning feedback into insights
- creating action items

It also recommends lightweight discussion structures such as:

- 4 Ls: loved, loathed, learned, longed for
- Sad, Mad, Glad

Important tactical excerpts:

- focus on "patterns and trends vs. one-time mistakes"
- "enter [action items] directly into your sprint planning or project management system"

This is a useful pattern for post-game review loops, even outside software teams.
