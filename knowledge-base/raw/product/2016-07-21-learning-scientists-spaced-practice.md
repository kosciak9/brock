# Learn How to Study Using... Spaced Practice

- Source: http://www.learningscientists.org/blog/2016/7/21-1
- Collected: 2026-05-26
- Published: 2016-07-21

## Excerpts

Definition excerpt:

"Spaced practice is the exact opposite of cramming."

"When you space your learning, you take that same amount of study time, and spread it out across a much longer period of time."

Practical advice excerpts:

- "Set aside a bit of time every day"
- "Review information ... but not immediately"
- "go back and study important older information to keep it fresh"

The article also emphasizes combining spacing with effective techniques like retrieval practice rather than simple rereading.
